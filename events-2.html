রাখি বন্ধন, ভারত সেবাশ্রম সংঘ

On the occasion of রাখি বন্ধন, we celebrated the festival with hundreds of children at ভারত সেবাশ্রম সংঘ. These children, who live away from their families due to financial or other hardships, rarely get the opportunity to enjoy such festivities. Our goal was to bring them joy, warmth, and a sense of togetherness.

The event was filled with love as volunteers tied rakhis on the children’s wrists, symbolizing protection and care. Alongside this, we organized interactive activities, shared stories, and distributed sweets, making the celebration even more special. The happiness on their faces was a testament to the power of kindness and community.

Beyond just a festival, this initiative was about spreading love and reminding these children that they are cherished and supported. It was a truly heartwarming experience that left lasting memories for both the children and our volunteers.
